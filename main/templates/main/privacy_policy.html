Privacy Policy

At igoCyprus, your journey and your data are in safe hands. Here is a quick summary of how we use your information:

  • To Get You There: We use your name, hotel, and flight details to coordinate your transfers and ensure your driver is on time.
  • To Enhance Your Stay: We use your location (only with your permission) to show you how far away your shuttle is and to suggest the best local excursions near you.
  • To Keep You Informed: We may send you real-time notifications about pick-up changes or booking confirmations for your activities.
  • Your Data stays with Us: We only share necessary details with our local trusted partners (like tour guides or drivers) to fulfill your bookings. We never sell your data to advertisers.
  • You Are in Control: You can turn off location tracking or update your profile details at any time directly through the app settings.

Privacy & Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

  1. Data Collection & Purpose: Knossos Travel collects Identity Data (Names, Passport info), Contact Data, and Travel Logistics (Flight/Hotel details). This information is used exclusively to execute your bespoke Cypriot itinerary, manage bookings, and ensure guest safety.
  2. Legal Basis (GDPR): We process data based on Contractual Necessity (to provide your tour), Legal Obligation (Cyprus Tourism laws), and Consent (for marketing or geolocation).
  3. Data Sharing: Information is shared only with essential local partners (Hotels, Transport, Licensed Guides). We do not sell data to third parties.
  4. Retention & Security: Data is stored securely and retained for 7 years to meet audit requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymized.
  5. Cookie Policy:
    • Essential: Necessary for app login and itinerary access.
    • Analytical: Used to improve app performance and user navigation.
    • Preference: Remembers your language (EL/EN/RU/DE) and display settings.

    Users may opt-out of non-essential cookies via App Settings.

  6. Your Rights: Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data ("Right to be Forgotten"). Contact our DPO at privacy@igocyprus.com
